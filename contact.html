Common Questions
How fast can you deliver? ▼
Most projects take 3–5 business days. Simple templates or analysis reports can often be done in 1–2 days. Rush delivery is available — just mention it when you reach out.
What formats do you work with? ▼
I work with CSV, Excel, Google Sheets, SQL databases, Power BI files, and most common data formats. If you're unsure whether your data format works, just ask.
Do you offer revisions? ▼
Yes — every project includes at least one revision round. I work closely with clients to make sure the output actually solves the problem before closing the project.
How does payment work? ▼
For smaller projects, full payment upfront. For larger builds ($100+), I typically work 50% deposit and 50% on delivery. I accept M-Pesa, PayPal, and bank transfer.
Can you work with my tools? ▼
I work with Looker Studio, Power BI, Tableau, Excel, Google Sheets, n8n, Make.com, Zapier, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Python, and more. If it's in your stack, I can almost certainly work with it.
What if I'm not sure what I need? ▼
Just describe the problem you're trying to solve. I'll help you figure out the best approach, give you an honest scope, and recommend the simplest solution — not the most expensive one.
